How It Works

  1. Begin Your Journey with Recarra: Getting started is easy. Simply upload a photo, scan your VIN, or enter a few vehicle details. Our intelligent system will immediately begin analyzing your car to prepare a real offer — with no obligations, and full respect for your time.
  2. Receive a Real, Personalized Cash Offer: Recarra’s AI technology evaluates your car based on its specs, condition, and current market trends. You’ll receive a firm, personalized cash offer — not a vague estimate. Because markets shift, offers are time-sensitive and may adjust based on demand, so we recommend acting quickly for the best deal.
  3. Schedule Pickup at Your Convenience: We work on your terms. Whether you prefer video confirmation, map-based scheduling, or a simple date and time, you get full control over when and how we collect your vehicle. Same-day pickups are available in many areas.
  4. We Handle Everything In Between: Worried about paperwork or titles? Let us help. Whether you’re missing documentation or unsure about the process, Recarra provides full support to verify ownership and ensure a smooth handoff.
  5. Confirm Your Final Details with Confidence: Before any vehicle is picked up, you’ll review the car’s condition, confirm your contact and location, and select your preferred payment method. Everything is transparent, respectful, and professional.
  6. Get Paid Instantly — No Waiting: As soon as the car is picked up, your payment is sent — typically within minutes. Whether it’s Zelle, Venmo, direct deposit, certified check, or cash, Recarra ensures your money gets to you fast and securely.
